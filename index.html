🏰 Shadow Realm

The Awakening

How to Play

  • Use ⬅️⬆️➡️⬇️ to move
  • Collect glowing orbs 🟡 for score
  • Avoid shadows with glowing edge
  • Reach target score to unlock next level
  • Walls block your path but guide your way
  • Press ⎋ or Pause to pause the game
